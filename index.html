Frequently Asked Questions

How will my item(s) be packaged? Each item is carefully wrapped with a generous amount of protective padding to ensure a safe and successful delivery. Depending on the item(s), we use discrete bubble mailer envelopes or standard shipping boxes.
Do you ship in the United States? Yes! We ship anywhere in the United States, including U.S. territories such as Guam and Puerto Rico.
Do you ship items internationally? Yes! We currently ship internationally; keep in mind though that we cannot verify addresses outside of the United States so please double-check the address you provide during checkout.
Are your minerals sourced ethically? Yes. All of our minerals come from well-trusted sources.
Do you offer returns or exchanges? No. However, if you receive a damaged item please contact us within THREE DAYS of delivery and send detailed pictures of the damage(s) so that we can properly find a solution for you.
Why haven't I received my item yet? We carefully package our items and ship within 1 - 5 days of receiving payment...sometimes the same day! That being said, once we release the package(s) to the USPS, the delivery process is out of our control. If you are experiencing a longer delivery time than normal, please contact your local Post Office first to see if they can provide details on the current location of your item(s). Keep in mind that during holidays, the USPS receives a high volume of packages and as such, delivery times may be slightly delayed.
